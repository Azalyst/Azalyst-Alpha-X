## 🧠 Strategy Architecture: Bollinger Band Breakout & Pullback

The flagship strategy implements a mean-reversion breakout framework utilizing Bollinger Bands (20, 2) to capture volatility expansions followed by structured pullbacks.

### 🟢 Long Signal Logic (Upper Band Interaction)

| Phase | Condition | Action | Risk Management |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Breakout** | Price closes **above** Upper Bollinger Band | Monitor | None |
| **2. Pullback** | Price retraces to **touch** Upper Band | Prepare Entry | None |
| **3. Trigger** | Price turns **upward** after touch | **BUY** | SL: Low of Touch Candle |
| **4. Expansion** | Price moves further above Upper Band | Hold Position | Trail/Static SL Active |
| **5. Exit** | Price re-approaches and **touches** Upper Band from above | **SELL** (Close Long) | Realize PnL |

**Visual Flow:**
```text
Price > Upper Band → Pullback to Touch → Upward Turn → ENTRY (Long)
   ↓
Price Runs Higher
   ↓
Price Returns to Touch Upper Band → EXIT (Close Long)
```

### 🔴 Short Signal Logic (Lower Band Interaction)

| Phase | Condition | Action | Risk Management |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Breakout** | Price closes **below** Lower Bollinger Band | Monitor | None |
| **2. Pullback** | Price rallies to **touch** Lower Band | Prepare Entry | None |
| **3. Trigger** | Price turns **downward** after touch | **SELL** (Short) | SL: High of Touch Candle |
| **4. Expansion** | Price moves further below Lower Band | Hold Position | Trail/Static SL Active |
| **5. Exit** | Price re-approaches and **touches** Lower Band from below | **BUY** (Close Short) | Realize PnL |

**Visual Flow:**
```text
Price < Lower Band → Bounce to Touch → Downward Turn → ENTRY (Short)
   ↓
Price Runs Lower
   ↓
Price Returns to Touch Lower Band → EXIT (Close Short)
```

### 🚫 The "No-Man's Land" Rule (Middle Band Protocol)

- **Strict Constraint:** No trades are initiated or managed while price action is confined between the Upper and Lower Bollinger Bands.
- **Rationale:** The middle zone represents noise and mean-reversion chop where edge is statistically negligible.
- **Post-Stop Loss Behavior:** If a Stop Loss is triggered, the algorithm assumes the price will revert to the Middle Band. The system **does not interfere** or attempt re-entry until a fresh Breakout-Pullback sequence is established at the bands.

## ⚙️ Scanner Configuration & Execution Parameters

The engine operates on a rigid time-cycle to ensure signal freshness and prevent over-trading.

| Parameter | Value | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Candle Timeframe** | `10m` | Primary data granularity for signal generation. |
| **Scan Interval** | `15m` | Frequency of full market scan. |
| **Lookback Window** | `10m` | Signal validity period. Conditions must match within the last 10 minutes at the time of the scan. |
| **Signal Expiry** | `Immediate` | If conditions are not met within the lookback window, the signal is discarded. |

**Execution Cycle:**
1. **Wait:** Pause for 15 minutes between scans.
2. **Scan:** Analyze market data for the specific `10m` lookback window.
3. **Validate:** Confirm if `Breakout + Pullback + Trigger` occurred strictly within that window.
4. **Execute:** Fire order if valid; otherwise, discard and wait for next cycle.
